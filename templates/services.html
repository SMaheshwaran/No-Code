vision

No-code development vision: Empower individuals to create software without coding, democratize access to technology, drive innovation, and foster collaboration for faster and more flexible application development.

future

No-code development's future is bright. Adoption will rise, platforms will advance, and collaboration will be emphasized. It will integrate with coding, focus on specific domains, and offer automation. Mobile app development will be simplified, and training will improve. In summary, no-code development will democratize software creation.

intelligence

No-code development simplifies software creation for non-coders using pre-built components and visual interfaces. It incorporates intelligence through automation and AI/ML features but has limitations for complex applications.
